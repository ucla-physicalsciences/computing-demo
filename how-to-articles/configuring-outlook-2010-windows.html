Configuring Outlook 2010 For Windows


The process for configuring Microsoft Outlook 2010 for use with departmental e-mail accounts is outlined below. Note that the process is the same for both @physics.ucla.edu and @astro.ucla.edu e-mail accounts. Both step-by-step instructions as well as a recorded screencast are provided for your reference.

Steps

  1. Open Microsoft Outlook 2010
  2. Click the File tab
  3. Click Account Settings -> Account Settings
  4. Click New
  5. In the "Add New Account" form, enter your full name, full e-mail address, and e-mail password and press "Next >"
  6. Wait for the settings to be automatically detected.
  7. Click Finish
  8. Click Close
