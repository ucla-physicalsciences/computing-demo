Zimbra Desktop Client

In addition to the standard desktop clients, Zimbra also offers its own client called Zimbra Desktop. It works with most Operating Systems (Linux, OSX and Windows)

The key benefit of this Email Client is that allows you to work off-line.

You can download the latest copy from here: http://www.zimbra.com/downloads/zd-downloads.html
