Troubleshooting Microsoft Outlook 2010 E-Mail Settings


In the event that your e-mail becomes inaccessible, it may be necessary to repair the Outlook 2010 configuration settings. The process for doing so is outlined in the video screencast and step-by-step instructions provided below.

Watch the video screencast

Steps:

  1. Click the File Menu
  2. Click Account Settings -> Account Settings
  3. Click on your e-mail account
  4. Click the "Repair" button
  5. Click "Next >"
  6. Click Finish

In the event that your e-mail account is still inaccessible after taking these steps, please submit a helpdesk ticket.
