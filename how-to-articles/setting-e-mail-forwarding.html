Setting Up E-Mail Forwarding


Enable Mail Forwarding

  1. Using your web browser of choice, browse to:

    https://mail.physics.ucla.edu for Physics Accounts

    https://mail.astro.ucla.edu for Astronomy Accounts

  2. Enter your username and password. Click the Log In button.

  3. Click the Preferences tab to open the General Preferences.

  4. Click the Mail icon on the left window pane to open the Mail Preferences.

  5. Enter a valid e-mail address in the Forward a copy to textbox. Click the Save button.


Disable Mail Forwarding

To disable mail forwarding, repeat the steps above and remove the e-mail address from the Forward a copy to textbox.
