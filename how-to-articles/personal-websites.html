Personal Websites


Personal websites are available to users who have departmental accounts. These personal websites are accessible at http://www.astro.ucla.edu/~username , or http://www.physics.ucla.edu/~username , depending upon your department.

To post a personal website, connect to one of the SSH Gateway servers (gateway.pa.ucla.edu) using your Physics & Astronomy email credentials, then create a "public_html" directory in your home folder. Any files placed in this folder will then be accessible on the web.

Currently supported filetypes include .html and .shtml.
